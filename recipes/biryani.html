Recipe for Biryani

Description

Biryani is a combination of rice, chicken/meat, herbs, and a variety of rare spices that will likely make your taste buds dance. Home to Pakistan, Bangladesh, and India, it has become a renowned dish worldwide.Today, we will share its original recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg chicken
  • 1.5 kg white rice cooked with a pinch of cardamon, cloves, cinnamon, salt, and bayleaf each.
  • 1 tbsp garlic & ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp red pepper
  • 4 tbsp biryani masala
  • 2 finely diced red onions

Steps

  1. Put half cup of olive oil in a large wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Now add 4tb spoon biryani masala and garlic paste in oil
  3. Add salt, red, and black pepper.
  4. Add bay leaf and half cup of water and cook them for 5 to 10 minutes
  5. Now add chicken and half cup of water and mix these ingredients and cook them well 20 minutes.
  6. During cooking of chicken make normal gravy for biryani recipe.
  7. After cooking the chicken with gravy Now Add boiled rice on chicken.
  8. Cover them for 10 minutes over Low-medium heat
  9. After 10 minutes Now mix them well that all ingredients mix with each other.
  10. In Hyderabadi biryani recipe, fried potatoes are used with chicken for extra flavor.
    11. Voila! Your biryani is ready.
