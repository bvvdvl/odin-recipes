Recipe for Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish produced from baked bread with cheese, some tomato sauce, and several other toppings for flavor. Originating from Italy, its recipe has spread worldwide. Some people prefer changing the ingredients to their liking. Today, we will share its original recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg Whole wheat flour
  • 1 packet of Mozarella cheese
  • 1 sashe of yeast
  • A small bowl of shredded and cooked chicken
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 cup of finely ground tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp live Oil
  • 1.5 tbsp salt
  • 2 basil leaves

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of water, olive oil, and yeast to the flour in a container.
  2. Knead the dough for a few minutes until consistent and leave for rising.
  3. Once risen, form a circular shape, and spread tomato sauce across the dough.
  4. Scatter some mozarella cheese and chicken evenly.
  5. For spices, add oregano, fresh basil leaves, and salt.
  6. Heat your oven to 400 Fahrenheit and let your pizza sit inside for 30 minutes.

    7. Voila! Your pizza is ready.
