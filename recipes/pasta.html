Recipe for Fettucine Alfredo Pasta

Description

The Fettucine Alfredo pasta is known for its creamy white sauce made from Parmesan cheese, cream, and other spices. It comes from Italy and is a quite easy recipe. Let us see how it is made!

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¼ tbsp pepper
  • 1 lb Fettucine pasta

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat water over high heat until boiling.
  2. Add salt to season the water.
  3. Once it is boiling, add fettuccine and cook according to package instructions.
  4. In a large skillet or pan, heat butter over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in heavy cream and let it reduce and cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
  7. Add half of the parmesan cheese to the mixture and whisk well until smooth.
  8. Save some pasta water. The pasta water is full of flavor and can be used to thin out the sauce.
  9. Toss alfredo sauce with fettuccine pasta and add half of the parmesan cheese. Once it is tossed, garnish with the remaining parmesan cheese.
  10. Add a little pasta water if it needs to be thinned out.

Voila! Your pasta is ready.
